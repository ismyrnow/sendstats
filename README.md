# SendStats

**SendGrid email stats, by subject line.**

Upload a SendGrid email activity CSV export and instantly see sends, deliveries, opens, and clicks grouped and ranked by subject.

## Usage

1. In SendGrid, go to **Activity → Activity Feed**, set your date range, and click **Export CSV**. SendGrid will email you a download link when it's ready.
2. Open [SendStats](https://ismyrnow.github.io/sendstats) and upload the CSV.
3. View your top subjects ranked by send volume. Click any column header to sort.

## Privacy

Everything runs in your browser. Your CSV is never uploaded to a server and no data is transmitted anywhere.
